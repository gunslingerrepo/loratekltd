Who We Are

IT and Cybersecurity
done properly.

Loratek Limited is a leading Information Technology and Cybersecurity solutions company proficient at consulting, integration, and strategic IT advisory services — offering customised services to small, medium, and large enterprises across different sectors in Africa.

Our objective is to maximise the opportunity for our clients to sustain their competitive advantage while eliminating the increased risks that accompany business dependence on technology solutions.

We work with organisations in financial services, energy, manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and government — delivering outcomes that match the complexity of their operations.
