Technology for Nigeria’s ports, shipping, and supply chain.

Nigeria's logistics and maritime sector is the backbone of the economy — and it runs on operational technology that demands real-time data, high uptime, and connectivity across multiple sites and stakeholders. We deliver the IT infrastructure and integration capability that keeps cargo moving.

Port & Terminal Operations

Systems that match the pace of port operations.

Port technology downtime doesn't just slow operations — it stops them, with immediate commercial and regulatory consequences. We implement and manage port management systems, vessel tracking integrations, and the secure networks that connect terminal operators, shipping agents, and customs authorities.

  • Port Management System (PMS) integration and support
  • Vessel Tracking System (VTS) connectivity
  • Customs and NPA API integration
  • Port operations network design and 24/7 management

Fleet & Freight Technology

Real-time visibility across your fleet and supply chain.

Fleet operators, freight forwarders, and logistics companies need real-time visibility to manage costs, reduce delays, and meet customer commitments. We build and integrate fleet management platforms, cargo tracking systems, and the operational dashboards that turn data into decisions.

Cybersecurity & Compliance

Protecting critical logistics infrastructure.

Logistics and maritime operations are increasingly targeted — ransomware against port systems and freight software has caused significant disruption globally and in West Africa. We assess your exposure, implement appropriate controls, and provide ongoing monitoring across operational and IT environments.

  • Maritime cybersecurity assessment and hardening
  • NIMASA and port authority compliance support
  • Ransomware protection and incident response planning
  • Staff security awareness for logistics operations
